# Remote Desktop Access Through VPN

## Objective

Allow secure RDP access to internal machines through the VPN tunnel.

---

## Scenario

A remote workstation connected through OpenVPN needed access to internal lab machines.

---

## Configuration

The following configurations were validated:

- VPN routing
- Firewall permissions
- Internal DNS resolution
- RDP service accessibility

---

## Validation

After successful VPN connection:

- Internal machines responded to ping
- RDP sessions established successfully
- Active Directory authentication worked properly
